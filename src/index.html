About our bussiness

Tokei was born from the shared passion of two lifelong friends who aspired to build a business in the refined and meticulous field of luxury watch trading at an international scale. Built on trust, expertise, and a deep appreciation for craftsmanship, Tokei connects discerning clients with exceptional timepieces sourced directly from Japan’s most reputable dealers.
Our mission is to provide a seamless, transparent, and personalized experience for collectors and enthusiasts seeking watches of enduring value and timeless elegance.
